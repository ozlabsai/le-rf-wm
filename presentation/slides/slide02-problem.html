Problem Statement

RF spectrum monitoring requires understanding the temporal dynamics of spectral scenes. Traditional approaches rely on hand-crafted features and threshold-based anomaly detection.

Goal

Learn a world model that predicts future RF spectral states and detects anomalies via surprise -- without hand-crafted features or explicit signal models.
