Why Model RF Environments?

DYNAMIC

RF spectra evolve continuously. Signals appear, shift, interfere, and vanish over time.

NOISY

Thermal noise, multipath fading, and interference create complex stochastic observations.

PARTIALLY OBSERVABLE

Limited bandwidth windows. No direct access to transmitter state or intent.

Goal: learn a world model of RF spectral evolution for prediction and anomaly detection.

Not classification. Not detection.
Modeling how the world evolves.
