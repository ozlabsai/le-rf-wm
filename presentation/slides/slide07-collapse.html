std(embeddings) = 0.000

WHAT HAPPENED

The encoder outputs the exact same vector for every input spectrogram. The predictor trivially copies it. Loss is zero. Nothing is learned.

THE TRAP

Loss looks good (near zero). Training curves look smooth. But the model has found the ultimate shortcut: ignore the input entirely.

This is representation collapse — the central failure mode of self-supervised learning without reconstruction.

Everything that follows is about understanding why this happens and fixing it.
