Key Lessons

1

Collapse is structural, not optimization

CLS token has zero variance by construction. No amount of training fixes a broken architecture.

2

Loss functions define which shortcuts exist

Every loss function creates a landscape of easy solutions. Your job is to eliminate the trivial ones.

3

MSE enables shrinkage. Absolute targets enable memorization.

Both are specific, diagnosable shortcuts with specific, targeted fixes.

4

Residual + L2-norm = real learning signal

Together they force the model to predict the direction of temporal change at unit scale.

5

Rollout training teaches composition

Not just one-step accuracy. The model learns that its outputs become its inputs.
